## What is Tiny Wisdom Cards?

Tiny Wisdom Cards is a small, family-run studio based in Tinkune, Subidhanagar, Kathmandu, Nepal (44600).
It makes bilingual, story-rich alphabet learning card decks for children aged 3 and above.
The cards are illustrated in Kathmandu and printed on heavy 350 gsm stock with glossy lamination and rounded corners.
The brand's mission is to pass on Nepali stories, scripts, and everyday values to young children — in a form they will actually reach for.

## Products

### 1. A–Z Ancient Wisdom Deck
- **Type:** English alphabet learning card deck
- **Contents:** 31 illustrated cards — 26 hero (alphabet) cards, plus mantra cards, story cards, and activity cards — and one A4 folded guide book
- **Alphabet coverage:** A through Z
- **Each card features:** An English letter paired with a legendary hero from the Mahabharata or Ramayana, a value that hero embodies, and rich cultural illustration
- **Example heroes:** A = Arjuna (The archer who learns to listen), B = Balarama (Strength and loyalty), G = Ganesha (Begin every story with attention), H = Hanuman (Loyalty as a daily practice), K = Krishna (Play, mischief, deep wisdom), N = Nandi (The gentle guardian of devotion), R = Rama (Patience over pride), S = Saraswoti (Goddess of wisdom and the arts)
- **Extra cards included:** Mantra cards (sacred chants with simple meanings), Story cards (engaging tales behind the legends), Activity cards (fun reflections for curious minds)
- **Price:** Rs 499 per deck
- **Availability:** In stock — ships in 2–4 days
- **Shipping:** Free within Nepal
- **Card stock:** 350 gsm, glossy lamination, rounded corners

### 2. Ka–Kha Sanskaar Lipi Deck
- **Type:** Nepali (Devanagari) alphabet learning card deck
- **Contents:** 32 illustrated letter cards and a folded guide book
- **Alphabet coverage:** Full Nepali alphabet — क (ka) through ज्ञ (gya), including conjuncts क्ष, त्र, ज्ञ and vowels
- **Language:** Nepali / Devanagari script, with English transliterations
- **Concept:** Each letter is paired with a Nepali value-phrase that the child can carry through the day. Example: क = कर्म गर (karma gara) = "Do your work"; ख = खेल्न जाऊ (khelna jaau) = "Go and play"; ग = गाउँ जाऊ = "Go to the village" (Roots); घ = घमण्ड नगर = "Do not be arrogant" (Humility); न = नमस्ते गर = "Say Namaste" (Humility); प = पाठ पढ = "Read the lesson" (Learning); य = योग गर = "Do yoga" (Balance)
- **Each card anatomy:** (1) Large Devanagari letter in classic proportions for tracing, (2) a value-word/phrase, (3) three illustrated companion icons sharing the sound (e.g., for क: kamala, kalash, kachuwa), (4) a folk-art border — paubha-inspired, mithila-flavoured — unique on every card back
- **Price:** Rs 300 preorder (retail price at launch: Rs 399)
- **Availability:** Preorder — ships June 2026
- **Shipping:** Free within Nepal
- **First-run perks:** First 200 orders receive a signed founder's note

## Our Story

Tiny Wisdom Cards was founded by a family in Kathmandu with a simple wish: to pass on the stories, scripts, and small daily values their grandparents gave them — in a form a four-year-old would actually reach for.
Every card is crafted with a distinct visual identity, tested by children aged 3–9.
The cards are curriculum-aware (aligned with early-grade Nepali language learning), beautifully illustrated with visuals rooted in Nepali culture, durable (glossy laminated), and made in Nepal (printed and shipped from Kathmandu).
